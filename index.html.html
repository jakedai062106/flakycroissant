Our Story

Baked with Passion, Served with Love

The Flaky Croissant brings a slice of Paris to Okemos. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to hand-craft each pastry using traditional French techniques and the finest European-style butter.

From our signature butter croissants to our delicate Mont Blanc, every item is made fresh daily. Pair your pastry with a perfectly pulled espresso or a creamy latte for the ultimate experience.
